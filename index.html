Shiqian Wu received the B.Eng. and M.S. degrees from the Huazhong University of Science & Technology and the Ph.D. degree from the Nanyang Technological University. Since September 2014, he has been successively employed as the distinguished professor of Wuhan university of science and technology (WUST), Chutian scholar, principal researcher of Hubei collaborative innovation center for high-performance materials and their applications, director of Hubei Province Key Laboratory of Intelligent Information Processing and Real-Time industrial systems, deputy director of institute of robotics and intelligent systems, and director of machine vision research office.

In July 2019, he was appointed as a distinguished professor and Chutian scholar in the school of Information Science and Engineering, WUST. He is currently a full Professor with the School of Information Science and Engineering.

Prof. Wu is a senior member of IEEE and Chinese Most Cited Researchers. Currently, deputy editor of J of Advanced Bionics, Biokinetics and Biocybernetics international Journal.
