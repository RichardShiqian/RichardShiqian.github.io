About me

  • From 1988 to 1997, professor Wu worked as assistant, lecturer and associate professor in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). He successively served as director of the machine tool research office of HUST and deputy director of the institute of advanced manufacturing technology.

  • From 2000 to 2001, he worked as a research engineer and researcher in the signal processing center of Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR).

  • From 2002 to August 2014, he worked as a researcher, senior researcher or scientist in the information and communication research institute of Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR).

  • Since September 2014, he has been successively employed as the distinguished professor of Wuhan university of science and technology (WUST), Chutian scholar, principal researcher of Hubei collaborative innovation center for high-performance materials and their applications, director of Hubei Province Key Laboratory of Intelligent Information Processing and Real-Time industrial systems, deputy director of institute of robotics and intelligent systems, and director of machine vision research office.

  • In July 2019, he was appointed as a distinguished professor and Chutian scholar in the school of Information Science and Engineering, WUST. He is currently a full Professor with the School of Information Science and Engineering.

    • Prof. Wu is a senior member of IEEE and Chinese Most Cited Researchers. Currently, deputy editor of J of Advanced Bionics, Biokinetics and Biocybernetics international Journal. He has served as editor of the Scientific World Journal, member of the IEEE multimedia communications technology committee, and member of the IEEE international conference on industrial electronics and applications technical committee.
